Our story started in 1980, Edgard Wild was a little 10 year-old boy who liked to entertain his friends in the classroom and to do unbelievable tricks.

He discovered the wonderfull universe of the circus when he was 15 years old, during a juggling's show, with his parents. Since this day, he used all his energy to make true his dream, to perform with the most popular acrobats, surrounded by animals more impressive each than the others.

So was born the « Wild Circus ». Today, The "Wild Circus" performs all around the world, in France, the country where his maker was born, in all the Europe, in the United-states of America, in Asia and in many other countries.

The "Wild Circus" offers shows which marvels childrens and adults, of every ages. You will discover unbelievable juggling's shows, acrobats, dressage of wild animals, dances of horses, clowns and an unique show made by the bigger human cannonball, the marvellous Ludwig Bullet.

Come and see us to discover our universe made to entertain you and your family into a dream of lights, wild animals and laughs.
